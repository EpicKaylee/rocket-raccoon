CONCLUSIVE SUMMARY

After weeks of research and interviews with FCI students and lecturers, here are our conclusions regarding these four programming languages:

Firstly, Python stands out as the most suitable language for web development and data analysis due to its simplicity and compatibility with other software.

Next, C++ excels in game development and finds common usage in embedded systems and applications where performance is paramount.

Furthermore, PHP also finds its place in web development for its user-friendly nature and wide support among web developers.

Lastly, Java is frequently employed in enterprise applications and Android development, appreciated for its cross-platform compatibility.
